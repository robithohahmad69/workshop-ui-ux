About ROSÉ

The Golden Voice

Park Chaeyoung, professionally known as ROSÉ, is the main vocalist of BLACKPINK and one of the most distinctive voices in K-pop. Born in New Zealand and raised in Australia, she moved to South Korea in 2012 to pursue her dream of becoming an artist.

Known for her unique vocal timbre and emotional delivery, ROSÉ has captivated millions worldwide. Her solo debut "R" shattered records, showcasing her artistry and musical versatility beyond the group.
